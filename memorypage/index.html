Biografy:

          Andrey Sklyarov studied at school No. 2 in Mytishchi. In 1984 he graduated from the Faculty of Aerophysics and Space Research of the Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology with a degree in research physics. 1984-86 engineer at the Central Research Institute of Mechanical Engineering (space industry). 1986-1989 - Deputy Secretary of the Komsomol Committee on Ideology (vacated position). 1989-1990 - leading specialist of the Personnel Department of the Ministry of General Engineering. 1990-1993 - Vice-President of the international aerospace society "Vzlyot", deputy. General Director of the International Aerospace Company "Vertical". From here onwards - free commercial activity in various positions. In 2001, Sklyarov headed the Foundation for the Development of Science "III Millennium", and since 2003 he became Vice-President of the League of Patient Advocates. A. Sklyarov participated in many research expeditions, traveled to many parts of the world where he conducted various studies. As a result, a series of popular science books and films devoted to the study of alternative history is published. He is the administrator of the Internet portal “Philosopher’s Stone Club”, and also became the founder and leading author of the “Alternative History Laboratory”, one of the large web resources dedicated to the mysteries and secrets of ancient history.
